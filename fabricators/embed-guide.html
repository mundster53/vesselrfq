How it works

Four steps to go live

1

Create your account

Register at vesselrfq.com/fabricators. Once your subscription is active, you'll receive your unique embed code by email.

2

Copy your embed code

Your unique code will look like this — it's already configured for your shop.

<iframe src="https://vesselrfq.com/app/embed?shop=YOUR_ID" width="100%" height="800" frameborder="0" style="border:none;border-radius:8px;"></iframe>
3

Paste it on your website

Paste the code anywhere on your website where you want the configurator to appear. Works with WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Webflow, or any custom site — if it accepts HTML, it works.

WordPress Squarespace Wix Webflow Custom HTML
4

You're live

That's it. Buyers who visit your page can immediately start configuring their vessel specs. Every completed RFQ lands in your VesselRFQ dashboard with a full spec sheet — nothing missing.
