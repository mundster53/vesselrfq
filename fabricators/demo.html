Why this is different

Built by someone who lived every side of this problem.

I've spent 36 years in industrial construction and manufacturing — food, chemical, petrochemical, pulp and paper. I've been the corporate project engineer writing the specs, the capital project manager managing the budgets, and the estimator at multiple ASME Code shops putting numbers on pressure vessels and piping packages worth millions.

At one point I was personally estimating $5 million worth of projects a month. I've quoted vessels in carbon steel, stainless, Hastelloy, Inconel — and I've supervised the installation of that equipment in the field. I was the first person to use the Tennessee-Tombigbee Waterway to ship a 50,000-pound Inconel 600 vessel to a manufacturer in Mississippi.

The thing that killed quote turnaround time then — and still does now — is waiting on component pricing from suppliers. Back in the early '90s I was already asking my suppliers if there was a way to get their pricing downloaded directly to my computer. Nobody had an answer.

VesselRFQ is that answer. A complete RFQ from the buyer's side so your estimator has everything they need to move fast — because I know firsthand what happens when they don't.
